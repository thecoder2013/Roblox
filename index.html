Roblox

Roblox, launched in 2006 by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel, is a massively popular online platform where users can create, share, and play games.
With over 230 million registered users, it offers a robust game development tool called Roblox Studio and an in-game currency, Robux, which developers can monetize.
Roblox fosters social interaction through friends lists, chat functions, and groups, and hosts virtual events and brand collaborations.
It emphasizes safety with content moderation and parental controls. Increasingly used in education, Roblox teaches game design and coding.
Publicly traded since 2021, Roblox generates significant revenue and supports cross-platform play, including VR.
Its cultural impact is evident on platforms like YouTube and Twitch.
